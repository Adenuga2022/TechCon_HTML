History

The first TechCon North America conference was held in New Orleans, Louisiana in 1996. The conference has since moved around the United States to accommodate the growing number of attendees. TechCon North America is a training and expo that features industry experts leading discussions on technical development and solutions

Mission

The mission of TechCon 2024 is to explore the future of technology, share insights, and collaborate on the latest trends. TechCon is a conference that brings together industry leaders, tech enthusiasts, and visionaries.
